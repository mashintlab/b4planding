How It Works

How It Works

  • Users purchase BIDS (credits) to participate in penny auctions.
  • Each bid increases the price by €0.01 and extends the auction time by 15 seconds.
  • The last bidder wins and purchases the item at the final bid price, often at a fraction of retail value.
  • The platform generates revenue through non-refundable bids, ensuring profitability.
  • Tokenization secures transactions and enhances transparency through blockchain integration.
